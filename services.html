Corporate Monthly Memberships

Our monthly corporate membership offers a dedicated concierge to provide the best that life has to offer. This membership includes access to your own in-house concierge Monday – Friday from 9am – 6pm, exclusive and unique offers ranging from travel, nightlife, wellness, restaurants, entertainment & sporting tickets as well as practical assistance. Our team of professionals will handle all of your staff’s personal needs during the day allowing them to stay productive at work. As Americans work harder and longer hours, they have less and less time to take care of “personal maintenance;” therefore, having someone handle your errands for you when you are working is quite attractive. Our corporate monthly memberships are a benefit to offer your employees and we offer a competitive employer rate giving your employees unlimited access to our concierge professionals at no additional monthly cost to them. Contact us at info@broadwayconcierge.com for more information on pricing and how to become a Broadway Member.
